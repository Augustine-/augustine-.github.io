TempGraph

One hot day, I wondered if opening my PC case and pointing a big fan at the interior would make a measurable difference on it's CPU and GPU temperatures.

A cursory glance around the internet revealed extant tools from the too-simple to the too-complex, nothing that brings up a time-series chart with a single click.

I also wanted to learn some C#. So: TempGraph.
